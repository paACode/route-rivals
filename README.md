# RouteRivals

**Where Cycling Meets Strategy**

> Transform your outdoor cycling into a real-time competitive multiplayer experience

## What is RouteRivals?

RouteRivals is a GPS-based real-time multiplayer competitive cycling game that bridges the gap between indoor virtual racing and outdoor GPS tracking. It's **Capture-the-Flag meets competitive cycling** - turning your outdoor rides into tactical battles where physical performance meets strategic gameplay.

## Project Status

**Current Phase**: Conception & Planning

### The Market Gap We're Filling

- **Zwift** gives you real-time competition, but locks you indoors
- **Strava** gets you outside, but competition is asynchronous (segments)
- **RouteRivals** delivers real-time outdoor competition with tactical depth

### ‚ö° Sprint Segments
Designated speed zones scattered across the map. The faster you blast through these segments, the more points your team earns. Risk burning matches early for points, or save your energy for objectives?
